Journalist covering sport, culture and student life.

I'm Ronan Sullivan, a journalist based in London, currently studying for an NCTJ Diploma at PA Media Academy.

My work spans non-league sport reporting, theatre and book criticism, and long-form student columns. I've written for the Enfield Dispatch, Impact Nottingham, and my own publication, The Line Is Good.

I'm drawn to stories at the intersection of culture and everyday experience — whether that's a play-off push at Enfield Town or a Brian Friel revival at the National Theatre.
