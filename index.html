Journalist covering sport, culture and the arts.

I'm Ronan Sullivan, a journalist based in London, currently studying for an NCTJ Diploma at PA Media Academy.

My work primarily spans non-league sport reporting, extensive theatre criticism, and deep dives into literary fiction. I write regularly for publications such as Anvil Heavy, the Enfield Dispatch, and my own platform, The Line Is Good.

I am drawn to stories at the intersection of local culture and large-scale storytelling — whether that's a crucial play-off push at Enfield Town, a modern French novel analysis, or reviewing a new opening at the Royal Court and National Theatre.

In the past, I also wrote long-form student columns covering campus life and culture for Impact Nottingham.
