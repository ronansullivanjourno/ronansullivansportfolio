SPORTS REPORT

HISTORIC EUROPEAN DEBUT FOR ENFIELD TOWN

Enfield Town kicked off their first-ever European Fenix Trophy campaign with a hard-fought 3-1 victory over reigning champions BK Skjold at the Queen Elizabeth II Stadium.

Despite facing heavy pressure on the counter from the pacy Danish outfit, Enfield broke the deadlock in the 37th minute when Sam Youngs slotted home a deflected effort. Skjold equalised in the 81st via Gorse, but a late Youngs penalty and Marcus Wyllie's stoppage-time goal secured all three points.
